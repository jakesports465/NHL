What is NHL Puck’Em?

Puck’Em is a weekly, game-based fantasy mini-league. Each Monday 08:00 CT a new week opens. You pick up to five NHL games; your score is based on game action — goals, shots, hits, blocks, goalie saves, PP/SHG bonuses, and end-state bonuses like OT/SO/close game.

Weekly Flow

  1. Browse the week’s slate (Mon→Sun).
  2. Lock in up to 5 picks before puck drop.
  3. Track live totals and plays in the viewer.
  4. Leaderboard finalizes after the week’s last game.

Why game-based?

It’s quick to play and every matchup can score: not just goals, but all the “grit” stats too.

Tip: Picks lock individually at each game’s scheduled puck drop. You can still pick later games in the same week.

How to Play

1) Sign in

  • Create an account, set a display name.
  • Forgot password? Use the link in the header.

2) Make Picks

  • Open the NHL tab, hit Open on any game.
  • Click Add to my picks. A ✓ “Picked” tag confirms it.
  • Max 5 picks per week. Each pick locks at puck drop.

3) Track Live

The viewer shows fantasy totals, bonuses, and live plays (scoring & recent). We also push results into weekly & season tables.

Picks & Strategy

Picking Basics

  • Max 5 games per week.
  • Each pick locks at that game’s scheduled start time.
  • You can still add later games after earlier ones lock.

What Scores Well?

  • High-event matchups: shots, hits, and blocks stack fast.
  • Tight games with OT/SO potential (bonuses stack).
  • Special teams impact: PP efficiency and SHG threats.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the week start?
Every Monday at 08:00 CT. That anchors the Monday→Sunday window.
Why did a goal not show in Scoring Plays?
We filter “no goal / goalie stopped / disallowed” messages and backfill from play-by-play when needed.
Are live totals final?
Totals finalize shortly after games end. If official stats are corrected, we reflect that on refresh.

Accounts & Privacy

  • Sign in with email + password; reset links supported.
  • Display names appear on picks and leaderboards.
  • We store only gameplay essentials: user id, display name, picks, and scoring results.

Troubleshooting

“No data yet” in Scoring Plays
Give it a refresh cycle; we also fall back to play-by-play. If a finished game still shows nothing, hard reload.
Times look off
We anchor weeks in Central Time; your browser’s locale can change how timestamps display.
